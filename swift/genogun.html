Genogun

Educational platform shooter, blending gaming with genetics

About the Project

Genogun is an innovative platform shooter game where you play as Rybo, a heroic ribosome defending cells from viral attacks. Armed with the Genogun, players must select the right genetic combinations (AA, Aa, or aa) to destroy viruses based on their specific genetic weaknesses.

This educational game makes learning genetics and molecular biology fun and engaging, perfect for students and science enthusiasts of all ages.

How to play

  1. Download Genogun from the Mac App Store
  2. Control Rybo, your ribosome hero, using intuitive platforming controls
  3. Analyze incoming viruses and identify their genetic code
  4. Select the correct gene pairing (AA, Aa, or aa) to neutralize each threat
  5. Survive as many waves as you can while learning genetics!

Game Features

Genetic Strategy System

Choose the correct genetic pairing (AA, Aa, or aa) to defeat viruses with unique effects

Precision Gameplay

Aim and shoot your Genogun with precision. Every virus has specific genetic vulnerabilities

Time Pressure

Defeat viruses quickly to avoid facing multiple waves at once

Educational Content

Learn core concepts of genetics and molecular biology while playing

Intuitive Controls

Jump between platforms and select genetic combinations with ease

Survival Challenge

Test your knowledge and reflexes across increasingly difficult waves
