Pace of Mind

Emotional intelligence meets task management

About the Project

Pace of Mind has been designed to help women get in tune with their emotions and use them as a powerful tool for effective task management. This innovative app recognizes that emotional states directly impact productivity.

Rather than fighting against emotional fluctuations, Pace of Mind helps users align their tasks with their current emotional capacity, creating a more harmonious and sustainable approach to productivity.

How it works

  1. Download Pace of Mind from the App Store
  2. Select factors that reflect your current emotional and physical state
  3. Let the emotion calculator analyze your inputs
  4. Receive personalized feedback and task management recommendations
  5. Plan your day in harmony with your energy levels
